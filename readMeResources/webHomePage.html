This is a community site dedicated to the game: Armored Core 6, developed by FromSoftware and Bandai Namco. This game focuses on building and piloting mechs/robots with a very detailed parts system. Expressing your creativity is a huge part of this game's experience, and the community already enjoys sharing their builds on sites such as YouTube. This site is a place for you to share your builds and experiences with the community.

Share your builds, find other build ideas, and join community-held events. You can find these builds under our Builds section of the site. If you wish to share your own builds, please use our Login page to make an account and share what great ideas you have!

Create, Experiment, and Share your findings!
